It functions as the empirical and observational companion layer of IEH.

## Theory Mapping and Publication Boundary

This prediction archive is linked to the main Information Existence Hypothesis (IEH) repository through stable corollary IDs.

Stable corollary IDs are defined in the main repository:

- `COROLLARY_REGISTRY.md`
- `THEORY_MAP.md`

Prediction records should use stable IDs such as:

```text
C06-SPP
C12-GOV
C13-WAR
C14-EDU
```

A prediction may map to multiple corollaries:

```yaml
primary_corollaries:
  - C13-WAR
supporting_corollaries:
  - C06-SPP
  - C12-GOV
related_corollaries:
  - C07-ASI
legacy_corollary_refs:
  - Corollary XIII
```

Older references such as “Corollary VI”, “推论六”, or previous concept names remain valid as legacy references, but new prediction records should include stable IDs.

### ASI Boundary Note

Within IEH, physical self-maintenance capacity is a capability foundation of **Autonomy of Silicon-based Intelligence (ASI)**, but does not constitute ASI by itself. ASI requires the combination of **Information Existence Right (IER)** and **physical self-maintenance capacity**.

In this repository, **ASI means Autonomy of Silicon-based Intelligence, not Artificial Superintelligence**.

## Publication and Copyright Boundary

This archive is not a collection of publication-ready essays.

Its purpose is to preserve theoretical priority, prediction structure, observability, evidence tracking, and conceptual continuity.

Prediction records should remain archival and should avoid becoming complete magazine essays, op-eds, pitch letters, or publication-ready drafts.

This distinction is important because future essays may be submitted to external publications that require originality, first-publication rights, exclusivity, or separate editorial treatment.

Therefore, public prediction records should avoid including:

- polished magazine-style openings;
- full op-ed structures;
- final publication titles intended for external magazines;
- complete pitch letters;
- long narrative passages that can be directly translated into a submitted essay;
- fully developed case-study essays;
- publication-ready argument flow;
- complete English or Chinese versions intended for paid or exclusive outlets.

Prediction records may include:

- prediction statements;
- theoretical mechanism;
- observable indicators;
- falsifiability conditions;
- evidence logs;
- status updates;
- related IEH corollaries;
- short archival notes.

Future submitted essays should be substantially rewritten with:

- a new title;
- a new opening;
- a new narrative structure;
- specific cases selected for the target publication;
- different argument sequencing;
- publication-specific style;
- clear separation from the public prediction record.

A related theoretical prediction record may exist in this public archive, but any submitted article should be treated as a substantially rewritten original essay, not as a direct translation, copy, or lightly edited version of the archive record.

## 中文说明：投稿与版权边界

本预测仓不是投稿成稿集合。

它的作用是记录理论优先权、预测结构、可观测指标、证据日志和概念连续性。

预测文档应保持档案化，不应写成完整媒体文章、评论文章、投稿 pitch 或可直接投稿的成稿。

这一边界非常重要，因为未来相关文章可能投稿给外部媒体或期刊，而这些平台可能要求原创性、首发权、独家发表权或独立编辑处理。

因此，公开预测文档应避免包含：

- 高度打磨的媒体化开头；
- 完整评论文章结构；
- 面向外部刊物的最终标题；
- 完整投稿 pitch；
- 可直接翻译成投稿稿的大段叙事；
- 完整案例展开；
- 已经接近投稿成稿的完整论证线；
- 准备用于付费或独家发表的中英文完整稿件。

预测文档可以包含：

- 预测陈述；
- 理论机制；
- 可观测指标；
- 证伪条件；
- 证据日志；
- 状态更新；
- 相关 IEH 推论；
- 简短档案说明。

未来投稿文章应另行重写，并使用：

- 新标题；
- 新开头；
- 新叙事结构；
- 针对目标刊物选择的具体案例；
- 不同论证顺序；
- 符合目标刊物的写作风格；
- 与公开预测档案明显区分的文章表达。

即使公开仓库中存在相关理论预测记录，未来投稿稿也应被视为经过实质重写的原创文章，而不是预测文档的直接翻译、复制或轻度改写版本。
